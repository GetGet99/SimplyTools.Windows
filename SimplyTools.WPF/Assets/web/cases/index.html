SimplyTools - Cases

Extra Info: Convert text to UPPERCASE, lowercase, swap case, or random case instantly.

This text case converter lets you quickly change the capitalization of any text without extra effort. Paste or type your text into the box, then choose one of the available transformations:

  • UPPERCASE – make all letters capitalized.
  • lowercase – convert all letters to lowercase.
  • swappingCASE – flip the case of each character individually.
  • rANdOmcAse – randomize the case of every letter for fun or stylistic text.

This is useful for editing documents, preparing social media posts, or experimenting with different text styles.

SimplyTools are tools run locally in your browser. Easily accessible from all your devices with internet connections.
Whatever data you input here stays within your device. No ads and none of your data are being sent out to the internet.
