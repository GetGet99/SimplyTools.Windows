The Quick Templates feature lets you browse built-in code snippets or add your own reusable templates. Streamline your workflow by copying and customizing snippets for any use case. All templates are managed in one place for easy access and editing.

Built-in snippets cover common patterns and best practices, while custom snippets allow you to tailor solutions to your needs. Perfect for developers who want to save time and avoid repetitive coding tasks.
